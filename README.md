# 📊 Madhav E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze e-commerce sales performance across different states, customers, categories, payment methods, and time periods. The dashboard enables businesses to monitor key KPIs, identify profitable segments, and make data-driven decisions to improve sales and profitability.

---

# 📌 Description

The **Madhav E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance by tracking revenue, profit, order quantity, customer purchasing behavior, and product categories. It helps business owners and analysts identify top-performing regions, products, customers, and seasonal trends through interactive visualizations.

---

# 🛠 Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Developed the interactive dashboard and visual reports.
- 🔄 **Power Query** – Cleaned, transformed, and prepared raw sales data.
- 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Created calculated measures and KPIs.
- 🗂 **Data Modeling** – Established relationships between tables for efficient analysis.
- 📈 **Interactive Visualizations** – Implemented slicers, bar charts, donut charts, and KPI cards.
- 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard preview.

---

# 📂 Data Source

**Source:** Sample Madhav E-Commerce Sales Dataset

The dataset contains transactional sales records including:

- Order ID
- Customer Name
- State
- Category & Sub-Category
- Quantity
- Payment Mode
- Revenue
- Profit
- Order Date

The data was cleaned and transformed using Power Query before building the dashboard.

# 🚀 Features / Highlights

## 📌 Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate thousands of transactions across multiple locations and product categories. Without a centralized dashboard, it becomes difficult to answer important business questions such as:

- Which states generate the highest revenue?
- Which product categories contribute the most sales?
- Which months are most profitable?
- Who are the highest-value customers?
- Which payment methods are preferred by customers?
- Which products generate the highest profit?

---

## 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard is designed to:

- Monitor overall business performance through key KPIs.
- Identify high-performing states, customers, and product categories.
- Track monthly profit trends.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Understand payment preferences.
- Support strategic business decisions with interactive insights.

# 📈 Business Impact & Insights

### 📍 Revenue Optimization

Identifies high-performing states, enabling businesses to focus marketing efforts and expand sales in profitable regions.

### 📍 Product Performance

Clothing contributes the largest share of sales, while Printers generate the highest profit, helping optimize inventory planning.

### 📍 Customer Analysis

Recognizes high-value customers for targeted promotions, retention strategies, and loyalty programs.

### 📍 Seasonal Trends

Monthly profit analysis reveals peak and low-performing months, supporting better forecasting, budgeting, and promotional planning.

### 📍 Payment Insights

COD remains the most preferred payment method, while digital payments (UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card) also contribute significantly, helping businesses improve payment infrastructure.
